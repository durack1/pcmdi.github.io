Report 62:  Vertical Correlations of Water vapor in GCMs

Sun, De-Zheng, Curt Covey and Richard S. Lindzen
      June 2000, 12 pp. 

      Abstract

      Correlations of tropical mean water vapor with its surface values have been calculated for all AMIP1 models and some of the AMIP2 models. The previously noted discrepancy between the GFDL model and rawinsonde data also exists for other models: the interannual correlations of water vapor with its surface values decline to much smaller values in observations than in the models. This discrepancy is reduced somewhat after the data from the models were sampled the same way as the data from observations, but remains large even at levels low enough to trust raw-insonde data. (See also Geophysical Research Letters, Vol. 28, No. 2, pp. 259-262 or contact Curt Covey  for reprints.)
